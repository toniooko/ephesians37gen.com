The Manifesto

WHO WE ARE

"We refuse to stay silent while a generation scrolls past a gospel they don't understand."

There is a generation of young people who love Jesus but find typical Christian content boring or disconnected. They have real questions, but no one is giving them answers that make sense. We exist to change that.

We focus on the critical 9-to-15 age gap—the moment intellectual resistance begins.

We aren't just telling stories; we are providing evidence. We aren't just sharing verses; we are teaching logic. We transform TikTok, Snapchat, and Instagram from minefields into mission fields.

OUR SPIRITUAL DNA

Grace-Formed

"Of this gospel I was made a minister according to the gift of God’s grace."

Calling isn't earned; it's given. This allows the quietest child to become the boldest voice.

Word-Rooted

The Bible is our spine, not a decoration.

We believe a 13-year-old can defend their faith with logic and evidence when rooted in the Word.

Power-Empowered

His power, working in us.

We don't serve in our own strength. The Holy Spirit is the engine of our creativity and boldness.

The Curriculum: Our 12-module system is built specifically for a digital generation. We teach where the Bible came from, why it can be trusted, and how to spot lies in culture with clarity and confidence.
